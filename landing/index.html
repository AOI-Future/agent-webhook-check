Your agent's webhook accepts a forged signature. Find out in one command.

A free, single-file auditor for the highest-frequency real bug in AI-agent tool callbacks. No dependencies, no network, stdlib only.

A webhook is the callback that lets an outside system trigger an agent action — a refund, a deploy, a fund movement. If the signature check can be fooled, an attacker drives your agent's hands. webhook-check runs four executable checks against your verifier and prints PASS/FAIL:

  • VT-D-050 / 051 — accepts a valid signature, rejects a tampered body
  • VT-D-052 — length-safe: a forged short signature must not crash the handler (DoS)
  • VT-D-053 — constant-time comparison: no timing side channel that leaks the HMAC
python3 webhook_check.py --demo                       # see it find a real bug
python3 webhook_check.py --verifier yourmodule:verify # audit your own
